  • 2-10

The game

The objective

The objective of the game is to lose all your cards. The last player who has cards left loses, he is the durak (fool).

The player with the lowest trump card is the first player allowed to throw cards (also called attacking). He can throw any number of cards to the player next to him, but they all need to be the same symbol. The next player should now start breaking these cards (also called defending). Both neighbors of the defending player can throw new cards onto the table, as long as the cards have the same symbol as any of the cards on the table and the player has enough cards to break every card. If the player can break all cards and the other players cannot throw any more cards (they allow breaking), it's this player's turn to start throwing cards. If the player cannot break all cards, he has to take the cards into his hands. The next player can then start throwing cards again to his next neighbor.

Trump

Trump is decided at the beginning of the game. The first card popped out of the deck decides the trump suit. A trump card can be used to break any non-trump card, or to break a lower trump card. Trump cards can only be broken by higher trump cards.

Breaking cards

To break a card, put a card on top of it with a symbol higher than the symbol of this card. To be a legal break, both cards must have the same suit, or the top card must be a trump card. The cards are ordered 2-10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace. After successfully breaking the cards, it's your turn to throw cards to your neighbor.

Passing cards

Alternative to breaking, you can pass the cards on the table on to your neighbor. You can do this when all the cards on the table have the same symbol, and also have (a) card(s) of this symbol in your hand. It is now your neighbor's turn to break the cards on the table + the card(s) you added.

If you have the trump card, you can also pass by using your trump card. This works exactly like passing regularly, but if you pass using trump, you don't lose your trump card.

Taking cards

If you cannot break all the cards or pass them, you have to take them into your hand. The round is finished and you don't get a turn to throw cards to your neighbor.

Cheating

Stealing the trump card

You can replace the trump card by one of the cards in your hand. This doesn't change the trump but you get the trump card to play with as a regular card.
